About Me

I'm a Computer Programming & Information Systems student who loves bringing my ideas to life using technology.

Since I was growing up, I started to develop an interest in creating websites. I learned about some Content Management Systems like Wix, but I always felt limited by the few things that a CMS allowed me to do. I then abandoned the idea of building websites for a while.

It was during my teenage years that I started taking Web Development seriously. I did some research and it didn't take long until I came across HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Although it seemed overwhelming at first, I started learning about these three technologies by my own. That was when I realized how much I like coding and decided to pursue a career in tech.
